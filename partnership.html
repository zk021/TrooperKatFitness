About Us

Given a choice, all of us would prefer to be healthy, fit and happy. But often a job, family responsibilities, lack of transportation or just life itself can put your fitness goals on hold.

TrooperKat Fitness will design a plan specifically tailored to your objectives – whatever those may be. Perhaps you just want to be able to go skiing or hiking without feeling sore or out of shape.

Or, maybe you’ve always wanted to drop a few pounds to fit into those special clothes that have been collecting dust in your closet. Or maybe you want play with your grandkids or simply are stronger for everyday life.
