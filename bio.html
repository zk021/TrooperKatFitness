Bio

  • The Founder of TrooperKat Fitness, Tim Hemer, has spent more than 55 years converting unique business concepts into successful start-up companies, designed to serve others and improve the environment in the areas of health, waste recycling, agriculture, real estate, transportation, franchising, the legal field and business development.

  • Tim’s abilities have earned him recognition within the business community as an innovator in a variety of industries with a special emphasis on marketing, business development and growth.
  • TrooperKat Fitness was founded in 2021. However, its roots go back to 1964 when Tim opened his first gym. Tim brings a unique background in business development to his team of fitness professionals and his for passion fitness and helping others. His innovative ideas have combined to create TrooperKat Fitness.
